# Restricted Data (P4) — AI Not Permitted

## What this means

This data is classified as Restricted under UC policy. AI tools — including Claude, ChatGPT, and all other AI platforms — **cannot be used** to process this data at this time.

This is not a limitation of the tools themselves. It is a policy requirement designed to protect individuals and the university from data breaches involving the most sensitive categories of information.

## What counts as Restricted?

- Social Security numbers
- Medical records (HIPAA-protected)
- Financial account numbers (credit cards, bank accounts)
- Authentication credentials (passwords, keys)
- Export-controlled research data

## What you can do instead

1. **Use a non-restricted subset** — Can you accomplish your goal using only the non-sensitive parts of the data? For example, use department names instead of individual names.
2. **De-identify first** — Work with your data steward to remove or mask restricted fields, then re-classify the data.
3. **Use traditional tools** — Restricted data can still be analyzed using approved, non-AI software within secure UCSD environments.
4. **Consult with IT Security** — They can advise on whether your specific use case has any approved pathways.
