# Working with Public Data (P1)

## What this means

Your data is publicly available. There are no access restrictions, and AI tools can freely process this information.

## Getting started

1. **Identify your data source** — Note the URL, database name, or file location
2. **Choose your AI tool** — Claude, ChatGPT, or other approved tools can all work with public data
3. **Start processing** — Upload, paste, or connect your data source directly

## Best practices

- Even though the data is public, always cite your sources
- Verify AI-generated outputs against the original data
- Be aware that combining multiple public datasets may sometimes reveal patterns that should be treated carefully
